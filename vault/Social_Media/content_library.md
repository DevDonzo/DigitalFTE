## Email Reply Templates

### Welcome Reply (New Contact)
```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for reaching out! I appreciate your interest.

I'm currently focused on [current priority]. Could you tell me more about what you're working on?

Looking forward to connecting!

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### Quick Reply (Known Contact)
```
Hi [Name],

Got your message. I'll look into this and get back to you by [date].

Cheers!
```

### Invoice Follow-up
```
Hi [Name],

Just wanted to follow up on the invoice from [date]. Let me know if you have any questions or need anything from my side.

Thanks!
```

## Approval Workflow

Before posting:
1. Claude creates post file in `/Pending_Approval/POST_[PLATFORM]_[ID].md`
2. You review and approve by moving to `/Approved/`
3. Action executes and logs to `/Done/`
